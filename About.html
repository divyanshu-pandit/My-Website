Who we are?

At AS Finance, we are a dedicated financial consultancy committed to helping individuals and businesses access the right loan solutions with ease and confidence.

We understand that financial decisions — whether buying a home, managing personal expenses, or expanding a business — are important milestones in life. Our role is to simplify that journey by offering clear guidance, reliable support, and access to competitive loan options through our network of trusted banks and financial institutions.

With strong partnerships across leading lenders, we carefully evaluate multiple offers to ensure our clients receive the most suitable interest rates, flexible repayment terms, and faster approvals tailored to their eligibility and financial profile.

At AS Finance, we believe in:
  • Transparency in every step
  • Personalized loan solutions
  • Long-term client relationships
  • Ethical and honest guidance
  • Hassle-free documentation support

Our experienced advisors work closely with every client to understand their financial goals and provide solutions that are practical, affordable, and aligned with their needs.

We don’t just process loans — we build trust, provide clarity, and stand by our clients throughout their financial journey.

Our Commitment

For Home Loans : We assist clients in purchasing, constructing, or upgrading their homes by offering loan solutions that align with their income, financial profile, and long-term plans — ensuring competitive interest rates, suitable tenure, and affordable EMI options.
